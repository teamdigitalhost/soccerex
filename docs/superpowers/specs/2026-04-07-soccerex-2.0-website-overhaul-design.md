**Date:** 2026-04-07
**Status:** Approved

---

## 1. Strategic Positioning

Soccerex is evolving from a 30-year events company into a year-round global football business platform. The website must communicate this transformation while preserving the trust and credibility of the legacy brand.

**Framing:** The 30-year track record is the hook. The platform story earns its place after the user has seen what Soccerex does today. Legacy first, then evolution.

**Mission statement (from deck):** "To Fuel The Global Growth Of The Football Community Through World-Class Events, Insights, & Partnerships."

## 4. New Vertical Pages (3 pages)

Each vertical page follows the same template structure but with unique content from the respective deck. These are "coming soon" pages with interest forms.

### Template Structure

1. **Hero** - Dark, NetworkNodes, vertical name + one-line positioning
2. **The Opportunity** - 2-3 paragraphs explaining why this vertical exists, pulled from deck
3. **How It Works** - Core offerings/pillars, displayed as cards or numbered steps
4. **Who It's For** - Target audiences listed
5. **Interest Form** - Name, email, company, role, dropdown ("I'm interested as: Rightsholder / Company / Investor / Speaker / Other"), message. mailto submission.
6. **Launch Timeline Note** - "Launching at Soccerex Amsterdam, May 2026" or similar

### 4a. Deal Network (/deal-network)

**Hero tagline:** "Structured dealmaking, not just networking."
**Opportunity:** Football's most important deals happen through relationships. The Deal Network converts attendance into purposeful, pre-arranged business outcomes.
**How It Works (3 offerings):**
- Curated Deal Facilitation: pre-scheduled 1:1 meetings matched by deal objective
- Roundtables & Deal Lunches: peer-level, Soccerex determines who sits at the table
- Year-Round Concierge: ongoing broker between events
**Who It's For:**
- Rightsholders (clubs, leagues, federations, governing bodies, player reps) - invited free
- Companies (exhibitors, brands, sponsors, tech providers, investors) - enrolled
**Digital Infrastructure section:** Included as a subsection covering fan engagement tools, digital ticketing, loyalty programs, sponsorship measurement. Positioned as a focus area within the Deal Network.
**Timeline:** "Launching at Amsterdam 2026. Complimentary for Amsterdam; commercial pricing from Miami onward."

### 4b. HerSoccerex (/hersoccerex)

**Hero tagline:** "Women's football is entering its commercialization era."
**Opportunity:** Structural market shift from development to commercial expansion. Media rights growing, institutional capital deploying, sponsor mandates expanding, league professionalization accelerating.
**How It Works (4 pillars):**
- Panel Participation: women-focused programming on investment, governance, leadership
- Commercial Visibility: presenting sponsors and category exclusivity aligned with women's sport
- HerSoccerex Connect: year-round webinars, roundtables, newsletter, membership community
- Representation & Media: interviews, highlight clips, editorial, content library
**Who It's For (8 leadership groups):** Women as leaders, federation decision-makers, commercial directors, board members, investors, media leaders, club owners, innovators & tech leaders
**Timeline:** "HerSoccerex programming debuts at Amsterdam 2026. HerSoccerex Connect membership launches ahead of Miami."

### 4c. The Pitch (/the-pitch)

**Hero tagline:** "Where football innovation meets institutional capital."
**Opportunity:** Football startups need structured access to institutional capital, not just exposure and mentorship.
**How It Works (3 tiers):**
- Tier 1 - Open Application (Free): merit-based, top 5 pitch on main stage, winner gets speaking slot + media exposure
- Tier 2 - Capital Access Track (Paid): curated 1:1 investor meetings, structured pitch rooms, private capital roundtables
- Tier 3 - Institutional Capital Layer (By Invitation): official Capital Committee with PE, VC, and strategic operators
**Who It's For:** Football tech startups with real traction, investors/VCs deploying in football, corporate innovation teams
**Timeline:** "The Pitch debuts at Amsterdam 2026."

## 7. Build Sequence

1. Nav restructure (Platform dropdown) - affects all pages
2. Home page (new platform section + verticals showcase)
3. Deal Network page
4. HerSoccerex page
5. The Pitch page
6. About page (inject mission + pillars)
7. Events page (add Miami 2026, Riyadh 2027, move MENA)
8. Footer updates (add vertical links)
9. Build, commit, push, deploy
10. **Phase: Section-by-section design evaluation** - dedicated agent walks every section of every page, rates quality, catches inconsistencies, fixes individually with atomic commits
11. **Phase: Comprehensive overview design evaluation** - separate agent does holistic pass across entire site for cross-page consistency, spacing rhythm, responsive behavior, animation coherence, nav/footer uniformity, mobile QA

## 8. Design System (existing, no changes)

- **Colors:** Navy #09203e, Gold #bfb170, Off-white gradient #f4f3f0 to #eae8e4
- **Fonts:** Space Grotesk (headings), Inter (body), IBM Plex Mono (labels)
- **Components:** NetworkNodes particles, PixelDivider wave transitions, scroll animations (fade-up, slide-left, slide-right, scale-up)
- **Patterns:** Dark/light section alternation, gold accents, hover lift on cards, staggered entrance delays
- **No emdashes** in any content
